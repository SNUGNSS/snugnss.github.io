### SNUGLITE-I CubeSat

> System
- 2U CubeSat
- Main payloads: L1/L2C GPS receiver x2
- Sub payloads:
   3-axis fluxgate magnetometer w/ deployable boom
- Mass: 1.9 kg
- Orbit: SSO 575km (LTDN 10h30m)​
> Frequency:
- UHF (437.275MHz) 
   * downlink : beacon, command-return
   * uplink : tele-command
- S-band (2405MHz)
   * downlink : housekeeping data, mission data
